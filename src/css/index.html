The Beatles were an English rock band formed in Liverpool in 1960. With a line-up comprising John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison and Ringo Starr, they are regarded as the most influential band of all time.

This website has many of the features I have learned in CSCC 1145 HTML class at CSCC. It displays a list of albums, shows a selected album's detail, and gives options among different audio and video files to listen to.
